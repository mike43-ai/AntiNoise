### Description (≈1850 / 4000)

```
Anti Noise turns scattered information into knowledge that sticks.

Save an article, drop a note, snap a screenshot — Anti Noise distills each capture into a Feynman-style summary, then automatically generates spaced-repetition flashcards from the ideas worth remembering. Stay in flow with the built-in Focus timer, and review just the cards due today.

— FEYNMAN AI SUMMARIES
Every capture gets a clear, five-part breakdown: a simple explanation, an analogy you'll remember, knowledge gaps to fill, real-world examples, and a deeper question to keep thinking. Powered by GPT-4o vision (URLs, text, and images).

— FLASHCARDS THAT WRITE THEMSELVES
The AI picks 3–15 cards from each deep-dive based on content density. SM-2 spaced repetition (Anki-classic) schedules reviews so you only see what you're about to forget.

— FOCUS TIMER, BUILT IN
Pomodoro-style sessions with wall-clock accuracy. Background-safe — the timer keeps running when your phone locks, and a local notification brings you back when the session ends.

— DAILY DASHBOARD
See today's captures, your streak, and what's due to review. One screen, no clutter.

— PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
Captures live on-device first (SwiftData). Optional Firebase sync mirrors across your devices. Your OpenAI key stays in the iOS Keychain — we never see it.

— SHARE EXTENSION
Send a link from Safari, share an image from Photos, drop text from any app — Anti Noise captures it without leaving the source app.

— FREE & PRO
Free: 3 captures/day, 5 AI summaries/month. Pro: unlimited captures and summaries, 7-day free trial on first launch. Pro monthly and annual options available.

Anti Noise is built for solo learners, knowledge workers, and students who want to absorb the web instead of drowning in it. Available in English and Vietnamese.
```
