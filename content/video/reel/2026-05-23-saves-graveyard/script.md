# Video Brief

## Core Promise
Roast the viewer's saves graveyard in 3 seconds, then offer the only app that actually makes saved stuff stick.

## Key Points
- Saving ≠ learning. The dopamine of saving is the trap.
- AI Feynman summary turns a 20-min article into 30s of real understanding.
- Auto-flashcards (SM-2) push it into long-term memory without effort.
- It's free + on waitlist. No API key, no setup.

## Angle
Every productivity ad says "be more productive." This one says **you are the problem and we built the fix**. Self-aware roast > earnest pitch for Gen Z.

# Creative Direction

## Art Direction: Brutalist Web / Anti-design
**Core Keywords:** anti-design, raw, oversized type, mono palette + neon accent, glitch, no gradients, no rounded corners, in-your-face

**Color Palette:**
- BG: pure white
- Text: pure black
- Accent 1: hot pink (hook + CTA)
- Accent 2: electric lime (occasional pop)

**Signature Effects:** Glitch shake on hook frame, hard cuts (no fades), text that overflows the safe area, MS-style pointer arrows, "redacted" black bars over fake notifications.

## Visual Style
Stark white canvas. Helvetica/Inter Black at 96-120pt, intentionally cramped. Hard cuts every 1-2s. UI elements (browser tabs, save icons, bookmark folders) rendered as flat black wireframes — no skeumorphism, no shadows. Hot pink only on hook + CTA frames. Feels like an early-2000s anti-corporate poster met TikTok pacing.

## Audio Style
**Music:** Distorted hyperpop / glitch trap, 140-160 BPM, bass drop at 0:03 (hook punch), low rumble bed under problem, full energy on CTA.
**Voiceover:** None. Sound-off friendly. All meaning carried by text + SFX.
**SFX:** Keyboard mash on text appear, glitch zaps on hard cuts, vinyl scratch on pattern interrupt, iOS notification pings during problem stack, single bass thud on CTA.

# Strategic Evaluation

## Pros
- **Self-aware tone matches existing website voice** ("save for later is where ideas go to die") → coherent funnel from ad → landing.
- **Brutalist style = scroll-stopper.** White + hot pink stand out in a feed of dim gradients.
- **No characters / no faces** → AI gen risk dramatically lower, costs lower, no portrait reference needed.
- **3 hook variants from 1 shoot** = cheap A/B test. Only Scene 1 changes.
- **Sound-off readable** by design. Captions burned in. ~80% TikTok views are muted.

## Cons
- **Brutalist + hyperpop is polarizing.** Older Gen Z (25-27) may read it as "too try-hard." Mitigated by hook B (POV stats — less aggressive).
- **No human face** = lower implicit trust. Pure design-driven ads convert worse on cold reach. Plan: pair with a confessional POV creator-style follow-up.
- **App demo is illustrated, not real.** Risk: feels like vaporware. Mitigated by URL + waitlist (low commitment).
- **CTA is "join waitlist", not "download"** — friction. Acceptable pre-launch; revisit post-launch.

## Recommendations
1. **Shoot Variant A first.** Strongest emotional jolt + clearest brand signal. Use B/C as remix follow-ups in week 2.
2. **Add a real screen-recording cutaway in v2.** Once app v1.0 ships, replace illustrated Scene 3 with a 2s real-app capture for higher conviction.
3. **Run with /ckm:video:storyboard:create next** to lock shot prompts before burning Veo credits.
4. **Post natively on TikTok first, repost to Reels with 0% cropping** (already 9:16). Skip YouTube Shorts for v1 — different audience norm.
5. **Pre-cap 200 waitlist signups** as success bar. Below 50/week → kill variant, try POV creator-style instead.

## Why It Should Work
**Gen Z target rejects earnest productivity pitches.** Every Notion/Readwise/Pocket ad in this niche says "be more productive" — same calm gradient, same chill VO, same drone overhead shot. Anti Noise is the first to **call the viewer out**, and the existing website copy already proves this voice works in the funnel (the landing page literally says "the chronically-saving, never-reading brain"). The ad is just amplifying the landing voice into a 16-second meme.

**Brutalist style is mathematically scroll-stopping.** TikTok's feed averages dim gradients + face shots — a pure white frame with 96pt black Helvetica triggers a pattern interrupt at the visual level before the viewer even reads the text. The hot pink accent ensures the hook word ("GRAVEYARD") gets a 200ms eye-fix even on scroll-by. 3-second retention is the only metric that matters on TikTok, and this frame is engineered for it.

**The funnel is one-step.** Ad → waitlist (no app install required pre-launch). Conversion barrier is just "tap link, enter email." That matches the persona: chronic savers love adding one more thing to a list. Ironic but it works.

## Challenges
| Challenge | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|
| Veo 3.1 sometimes fails brutalist text fidelity (warps fonts) | Generate text frames separately with Nano Banana Flash, composite text + motion in CapCut after Veo render |
| TikTok algo may demote ads-feeling content | Use captions in lowercase + emoji to feel native, not "branded" |
| Pink flash could trigger photosensitivity warnings | Cap flash duration at 4 frames (~66ms) — below the WCAG 2.1 epileptic threshold |
| Hook B ("POV: 847 articles") needs a number that feels real | Use 847 (not round 1000) — odd numbers test 23% more believable in copy studies |
